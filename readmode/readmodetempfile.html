2 Nebraska football support staff members no longer with program

Two members of Matt Rhule’s first-year support staff are no longer with Nebraska’s football program.

Omar Hales, NU’s director of player personnel for high schools, and C.J. Cavazos, NU’s director of football relations, are no longer listed on the Huskers’ staff directory for football and their online biographies refer to their work in the past tense.

Hales, a key part of Nebraska’s recruiting operation, had worked with Rhule at three previous stops — Temple, Baylor and the Carolina Panthers — before taking the NU job.

Cavazos oversaw administration of the Huskers’ walk-on program and served as a contact for high school football coaches. Cavazos had previously run his own recruiting company in Texas.
